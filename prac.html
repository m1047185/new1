Mindtree Limited

Mindtree Limited is a multinational
information technology and outsourcing
company headquartered in Bengaluru,
India and New Jersey, USA.

History

In August 1999, Mindtree Consulting Private Limited
was founded by ten IT professionals of which three
of them invested through an entity incorporated in Mauritius.

Employees

Mindtree has a total of 19,000 employees as of August 2018,
of which 30% were women.Its workforce consists of employees
representing 55 nationalities working from various offices across the globe.
